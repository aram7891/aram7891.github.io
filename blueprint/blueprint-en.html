PILLAR 01

Radical Prioritization

Your energy is a finite resource. Learn to protect it.

Your real 20%

Identify the 2 or 3 things that, if you do them, completely change your day and your future.

Immediate action:

Open a note and write: “If I could only do 3 things this week that really matter, they would be…”

The Strategic “No”

Every well-placed “no” gives you back time, energy, and attention.

Immediate action:

Review your schedule for the next 7 days and cancel or delegate one single thing that is not part of your real 20%.

PILLAR 02

Action Systems

Motivation goes up and down. Systems sustain you.

Mental Download

Get everything out of your head so it stops taking up mental space.

Immediate action:

Before going to sleep, write on a piece of paper or note everything that’s swirling in your mind (no filter). Tomorrow you decide what to do with each thing.

Energy Blocks

Protect 90 minutes where you do only one important thing with zero distractions.

Immediate action:

Choose one task from your real 20% and block it tomorrow morning in your calendar as “Deep Work – Do Not Disturb”.

Day Closer

Decide tomorrow before closing today. That way your mind truly rests.

Immediate action:

Every night choose only one thing that will be your number 1 priority for the next day. Write it down and close the agenda.
